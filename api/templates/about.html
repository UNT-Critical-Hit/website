Join or Start a Campaign

Join an existing campaign or start your own within minutes. With over 50 active players, we have plenty of campaigns to choose from, ranging from virtual to hybrid to in-person. 

Meet Likeminded Students

Meet other students interested in TTRPGs and form friendships. Talk in our Discord, attend hangouts, and have fun. 

Weekly Hangouts
